Skills Overview

Design Systems

Create systematic approaches towards designing products. Visual elements, components, templates and screens, designed with reusability and standardization. Design systems expedite processess, track product growth and drive realistic goals.

Prototyping

After completion of wireframes and mockups, create prototypes that mimic experiences for testing and experimentation purposes. Prototypes provide insights into the way users may interact with products.

User Interface Design

Design of interface elements users will innteract with while interacting with product. Through data from research and experimentation - information architecture, visual elements and interactions are created in a way that is easy to understand.

Qualitative Research

Engaging with users and research participants to gain an understanding of motivations and pain-points associated with a product. Qualitative research is important for developing hypotheses and cross-referencing quantitative data.

Wireframing

Low to mid fidelity wireframes are used to understand the way information architecture will be displayed to the user. Wireframing is important for visualizing the information architecture to be displayed to users.
