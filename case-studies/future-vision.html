Background

What is it?

Future Vision was a research and design project completed by myself and two fellow HCI students at Drexel University. The steps we took illustrate some of the methods human-centered design professionals may tackle when brainstorming a new project or idea. By practicing empathy, ideation and testing, we were able to obtain valuable qualitative insights into the way people travel and the variables that negatively affect their travel plans.

My Role

I assisted the team in conducting qualitative interviews, participated in group brainstorming and brain-netting session, and was the UI designer.

Empathy

Assumptions and Corrections

1) Our Assumption

The group discussed our experiences with travel, as a broad term, as in going from "Point A" to "Point B". As a group, we surmised that traveling and navigating was, for the most part, unburdened by intense feelings.

2) Other Opinions

Over the course of three days, group members individually conducted qualitative interviews with participants via Zoom. Interviews were approx 30 minutes in length, with participants ranging in backgrounds, career statuses and ages.

3) Feedback

For many people, the act of planning out a trip and ensuring the trip goes accoridng to plan, can at times feel overwhelming.We found, no matter how brief the trip may be, traveling isn't easy for evreybody. Those working multiple jobs or those with toddlers and/or children are two examples of where traveling can be stressful.

4) Problem Statement

Addressing the stressors that occur during and planning for a trip is where we redirected our attention. The problem statement decided was, "People are overwhelmed by the variables that can affect their travels".

Properties

Data Properties & Object Properties

Data Property / "ABV", "Brewery"

Data properties add context to individuals. They are definitions or resources for additional data about individuals. Data properties, "ABV" and "Brewery", were added to individuals classified as Beer Names. In the image (DP-1) the data property assertions tell us that Black Toad (Beer Name) has an ABV of 5.9 and the brewery is Back Toad Brewing Company in California.

(DP-1), Beer Name's data properties

Object Property / "is_a"

Object Properties describe relationships between individuals. They connect instances togethor. In the image (OP-1), object-property, "is_a" is describing the relationship between Black Toad (Beer Name) and English Brown Ale (Beer Style) - Black Toad "is a(n)" English Brown Ale.

Wrap Up

Conclusion

What did I walk away with?

While I may not have learned a lot about Blue Moon, or its ingrediants, nor have I walked away with Blue Moon defending abilities, I have learned a lot of information about other beers offered at Trader Joe's. I've also learned how to design domain ontologies in Protege. Going forward, I may continue adding to this ontology with additional properties and possibly restructuring the beer styles into subclasses of "TJ Beers"
