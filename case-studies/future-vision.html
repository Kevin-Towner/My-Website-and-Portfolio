Future Vision

Introduction

Background

Future Vision is a project that was completed in December, 2020, by myself and two other students at Drexel University. Future Vision was our captone project for INFO-508, Information Innovation Through Design Thinking.

Purpose

The group originally decided to design a navigation-based application. Upon the completion of early steps in the design process, the group decided to focus on the stress and anxiety-inducing factors that can happen during a trip. The problem statement we decided on was, People are overwhelmed by the variables that can affect their travels"

Overview

  • Role: Researcher, UI Designer
  • Time: 10.20 - 12.20
  • Add'l: Prototype and Design Document

Process

Empathy

Prior to conducting any research, the group reflected on personal experiences relating to planning or going through the process of taking a trip. As a whole, the group surmised that traveleing is, for the most part, unburdened by intense feelings. From an empathetic approach, the group decided to interview a total of 8 people in loosely-scripted interactions to yield transparent and genuine information on the way others view navigation.
