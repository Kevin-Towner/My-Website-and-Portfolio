Get to Know Me

Professional

I work at AIG, for the Eastern Zone's Op Services Team. I manage multiple policy-related transactions such as: renewal and issuance processing, the communication of policy cancellations and the documentation of various lines of communication between various operational teams.

I'm driven by the subtleties of accomplishing a goal that encompasses interconnected teams and processes. From my work experiences and enthusiasm for design and studies in human-computer interaction, I'm interested in a career where I can make an impact on productivity and quality via thoughtful and human centered design.


Personal

I'm currently in New Jersey, where I have lived the majority of my life and I love it. Sometimes I wonder what food items I would miss if I ever decided to move out of state - pizza, bagels, etc. For the first two and a half decades of my life I never really knew what I wanted to do or who I wanted to be.

Over the last few years, I have spent a considerable amount of time learning new skills, figuring out what I want out of my life, and redefining the way I view work. I work for AIG's Eastern Zone Operations Team and am attending Drexel University, as a graduate student, studying Human-Computer Interaction. For fun, I enjoy running, muay thai, boxing, video games, netflix/hulu and listening to music.
