Why the Gundam logo?

Newtype and empathy

The term "newtype" is from Japanese anime series, Mobile Suit Gundam

Newtypes are people who have reached the next stage in the evolution of humans. They are said to be better adapted to living in outer space by possessing telepathic abilities and heightened mental awarenesses - sixth sense. Not only do newtype abilities make it possible for newtypes to become better mobile suit pilots, but they also have abilities surrounding telepathic communication.

While newtypes quickly became exploited for their talents by militaries and governments, they play a major role in the Gundam Universe. They are known for being able to accomplish near-impossible feats and change the course of the One Year War (a war between the Earth Federation and the Zeon), and it is because of their exceptional empathetic abilities.


Empathy and design

Empathizing is the first step in human-centered design

Putting preconceived notions to the side to learn, observe, and/or engage with other people is a challenging task. Empathy isn't some property that should be shrugged off as something some people innately have or not.

I believe empathy is a skill, which can be developed over time with practice and self-reflection. It also happens to be the key to understanding the very people design thinkers are designing for. I aim to design products that address human needs. I want to design elements that grow with time and simultaneously address the needs of users and organizations.
