Research & Design

Future Vision (Mobile App Design)

Worked with a multifaceted team, of fellow students at Drexel University, to design a mobile product aimed at streamlining the various decisions and variables that effect people's travels. The team (Group 4) worked togethor as well as independently; conducting qualitative research, brainstorming various ideas and designing wireframes and low to mid fidelity mockups. Group 4 completed the assignment with a final design document and an interactive prototype, with its own set of scenerios, illustrating a use-case based off the group's qualitative insights.

Course: Information Innovation Through Design Thinking (INFO 508)
Members: Kim Lee, Elizabeth Worsley, Kevin Towner
Time Frame: October, 2020 - December, 2020
