Introduction

Pixel art

When drawing a blank on creative ideas, I always feel tackling side challenges is a great way to gain momentum for creative inspiration. I've found pixel art to be, not only an activity that sparks creativity, but something fun and engaging to learn. While one may not have as many tools as somebody engaged in another form of digital art, pixel art teaches you how to do more with less.

Why the science fiction angle?

The science fiction and future cyberpunk angle allows for maximum creativity, bordering on incomprehensible. Similar to brainstorming, ideas revolving around the pixel art focus on quantity over quality and are not held to high levels of scrutiny.

Cyberpunk and science fiction are awesome because they tend to incorporate retro aesthetics, fantastical stakes, and deep subject matter. Let's just roll with it!

Electric Frogs

Artificial animals as a form of habitat control

Synthetic frogs are commonly used to deal with the spread of insects in rural areas. While many city residents prefer the use of synthetic cats or other mammalian predators to deal with the growing insect population, synthetic frogs have proven to be more useful in areas that foster naturalistic biodegration.
